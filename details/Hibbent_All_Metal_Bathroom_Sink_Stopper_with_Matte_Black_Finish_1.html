Hibbent All Metal Bathroom Sink Stopper

Say goodbye to clogged drains and frustrating water leaks with the Hibbent sink stopper. Designed for effortless use and long-term reliability, this stopper combines functionality with modern aesthetics to elevate your bathroom experience.

Key Features

  • Universal fit: Compatible with most US bathroom sinks and washbasins having drain hole sizes between 1.1'' and 2.3''.
  • Premium all metal: Built with corrosion-resistant materials for enhanced durability and a sleek matte black finish.
  • Anti-clogging basket core: Effectively traps hair, rings, and jewelry, preventing blockages and maintaining clean plumbing.
  • Sturdy bounce cap: Offers strong rebound and quick sealing for seamless transitions between use and drainage.
  • Easy installation & cleaning: No tools or plumber fees needed, making it simple to maintain a hygienic sink area.

Why Choose Hibbent?

The Hibbent all metal bathroom sink stopper is a perfect upgrade for those who want a durable, stylish, and easy-to-use alternative to traditional plastic drain plugs. Its reliable sealing mechanism prevents water from spilling, while the black finish complements modern bathroom designs with a touch of sophistication.

Whether you're handling everyday sink tasks or need a dependable stopper for special occasions, Hibbent delivers superior performance and convenience. The combination of high-quality materials and intuitive design ensures that this stopper is as reliable as it is easy to care for, making it a must-have for any homeowner or DIY enthusiast.
