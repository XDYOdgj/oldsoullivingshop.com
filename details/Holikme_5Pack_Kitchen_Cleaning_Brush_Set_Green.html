Holikme 5Pack Kitchen Cleaning Brush Set

This comprehensive kitchen cleaning brush set is designed to keep your kitchen sparkling clean with ease. Product Highlights

  • Includes 5 versatile cleaning brushes for multiple kitchen tasks
  • Soft dish scouring pad for gentle surface cleaning without scratches
