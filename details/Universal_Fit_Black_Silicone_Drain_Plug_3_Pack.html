Universal Fit Black Silicone Drain Plug 3 Pack

Enhance your home's hygiene and functionality with this durable, reliable, and stylish drain plug set. Made from high-quality silicone, these plugs are built to last without cracking, fading, or losing their flexibility. Their anti-aging and anti-corrosion properties make them perfect for use in wet areas like bathrooms, kitchens, and laundry rooms.

Each drain plug features a secure multi-layer threading system that ensures a tight fit and effective sealing. This prevents unwanted odors, pests, and water leaks from entering your drains. Whether you're dealing with a washing machine drain, bathtub outlet, or floor drain, these plugs are designed to provide long-lasting performance and a modern, clean look.

  • High-quality silicone material for durability
  • Anti-aging and anti-corrosion properties for long-term use
