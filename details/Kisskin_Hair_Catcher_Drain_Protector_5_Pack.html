Kisskin Hair Catchers: Keep Your Drains Clean and Clog-Free

Ditch the hassle of hair clogs with Kisskin Hair Catchers. These reliable drain protectors are crafted from durable thermo plastic, ensuring long-lasting performance without cracking or fading over time. Whether you're in a bathroom, kitchen, or laundry room, they help prevent unwanted buildup

Why Choose Kisskin Hair Catchers?

  • Designed with a non-slip base that keeps them firmly in place, preventing accidental displacement
  • Available in a 5-pack with a range of colors, so you can match your home's style while having spare options for different areas
  • Easy to install and remove, making maintenance a simple task
  • Effortlessly trap hair and debris before they make their way down the drain
  • Perfect for households with pets, kids, or anyone looking to avoid costly plumbing repairs

Versatile Drain Protection

Kisskin Hair Catchers are compatible with flat and open drains. They are not suitable for pop-up types, making them ideal for use in bathroom sinks, showers, bathtubs, laundry drains, and kitchen sinks. With their simple yet effective design, these hair catchers are your best bet for a cleaner home
