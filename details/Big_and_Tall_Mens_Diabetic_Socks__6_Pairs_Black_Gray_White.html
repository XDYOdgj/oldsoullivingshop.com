Big and Tall Men's Diabetic Socks

These diabetic socks are crafted with a blend of 80% cotton, 15% polyester, and 5% spandex to offer a perfect balance of softness, breathability, and support. Designed to keep your feet cool and dry throughout the day, they are an ideal companion for anyone who spends long hours in shoes or socks, including those with diabetes.

With therapeutic cushioning, these socks provide comfort and help reduce swelling. Their lightweight, non-binding design supports better blood circulation and is especially beneficial for individuals with circulatory issues or neuropathy. The loose fit also makes them easy to wear over swollen ankles, ensuring convenience without compromise.

Whether you're at work, running errands, or lounging at home, these socks are built to last and provide dependable support. They are perfect for everyday use and make a great gift for loved ones who value health and comfort. Now available in a pack of 6 pairs, you can enjoy consistent quality and better foot health without the hassle of frequent replacements.
