Lithonia Lighting Quantum LED Emergency Light

Ensure safety and clarity during power outages with the Lithonia Lighting Quantum LED Emergency Light. This advanced emergency light fixture is designed for reliability, durability, and ease of maintenance, making it the perfect choice for commercial and industrial environments.

Key Features

  • Equipped with two high-performance LEDs, each rated at 1.2 watts, for a bright 220 lumens output.
  • Delivers consistent linear illumination, providing clear visibility in critical areas.
  • Includes a built-in lithium phosphate battery that offers up to 3 hours of backup power, ensuring you're never in the dark.
  • Features intelligent self-diagnostic technology to identify issues quickly and efficiently.
  • Supports remote testing functions, simplifying maintenance and reducing downtime.
  • Constructed with a durable, flame-resistant white thermoplastic housing that also offers impact, scratch, and corrosion resistance.

Where You'll Need It

Whether you're managing a commercial building, an industrial facility, or any space where reliable emergency lighting is crucial, this LED emergency light provides peace of mind. Its low-profile design blends effortlessly into modern interiors, enhancing aesthetics without compromising function.

Built for Longevity

Designed for maximum performance, the Quantum LED Emergency Light features a long LED lifespan of up to 10 years, reducing the need for frequent replacements and lowering long-term costs. The combination of high-quality components and smart technology ensures consistent operation, even when the power goes out.

Ideal for both permanent and temporary lighting needs, this remote test emergency light is a reliable component of your emergency lighting system, crafted to meet the highest standards of safety and efficiency.
