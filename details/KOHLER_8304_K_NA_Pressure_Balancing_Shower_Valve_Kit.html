KOHLER 8304-K-NA Pressure Balancing Valve Kit

Elevating your shower experience, this KOHLER pressure balancing valve kit is engineered for precision, safety, and effortless installation. The innovative design ensures that your water temperature remains steady, even when there are fluctuations in pressure, providing a consistently comfortable and soothing shower environment.

Key Features

  • Stable water temperature with advanced pressure balancing technology
  • Universal NPT and direct sweat inlets/outlets, ideal for any bathroom configuration
  • High-flow capacity of 5 GPM and 45 psi handling for optimal water output
  • Single lever control for intuitive temperature adjustments and anti-scald protection
  • Durable forged brass construction, resistant to corrosion and built to last

Why Choose This Kit?

This valve system is a perfect upgrade option for homeowners looking to enhance both comfort and safety in their bathroom. Its easy installation process and versatile design make it suitable for replacing or upgrading your existing shower valve with minimal effort. Whether you're working with a standard, back-to-back, or thin wall setup, this product adapts to your needs, ensuring a seamless and efficient installation experience.

Customer Value
